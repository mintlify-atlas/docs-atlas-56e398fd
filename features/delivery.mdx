## Fast and convenient delivery

Once your custom Game of Thrones intro is rendered, it's delivered directly to your inbox. No need to wait on a webpage or check back later - you'll receive an email notification when your video is ready to download.

<Info>
Your video is delivered via email shortly after you submit your customization request. Delivery time varies based on the selected resolution and current processing queue.
</Info>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your customization">
    Fill in your title text, end text, email address, and select your preferred resolution on the creation page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rendering begins">
    Our system immediately begins rendering your custom intro with professional-quality encoding and the authentic Game of Thrones style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email notification">
    You'll receive an email with a download link to your personalized video once rendering is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and enjoy">
    Click the link in your email to download your video and start using it right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Make sure to provide a valid email address when creating your intro. Double-check for typos to ensure you receive your video.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting delivery issues

If you haven't received your video:

1. **Check your spam folder**: Sometimes delivery emails are filtered incorrectly
2. **Verify your email address**: Make sure you entered it correctly when submitting your request
3. **Wait a bit longer**: During peak times, processing may take longer than usual
4. **Check your inbox**: Search for emails from the Game of Thrones Intro Creator service

<Tip>
Add the sender email to your contacts to ensure future deliveries go straight to your inbox.
</Tip>
