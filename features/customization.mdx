## Make it your own

The Game of Thrones Intro Creator lets you transform the iconic opening sequence into something uniquely yours. Whether you're creating content for your channel, celebrating a special occasion, or just having fun, you have full control over the text that appears in your intro.

<Note>
Your customizations are rendered using the authentic Game of Thrones intro style, complete with the original music and visual effects.
</Note>

## Customization fields

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Title text" icon="heading">
    The main text that appears at the beginning of your intro. This is typically displayed in the iconic Game of Thrones font and style.
  </Card>
  <Card title="End text" icon="message">
    The closing text that appears at the end of your intro sequence, perfect for credits, messages, or additional personalization.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Keep your text concise for the best visual impact. The Game of Thrones intro format works best with shorter, punchy phrases.
</Tip>

## Text formatting guidelines

While you can enter any text you want, here are some tips to get the best results:

- **Length**: Shorter text displays more prominently and is easier to read
- **Capitalization**: All caps can create a more dramatic effect
- **Special characters**: Standard punctuation and special characters are supported
- **Multiple lines**: Use your text creatively - the renderer handles formatting automatically

<Info>
Your custom text is rendered with the same cinematic quality as the original series, ensuring a professional look every time.
</Info>
