## Professional quality rendering

Your custom Game of Thrones intro is rendered using professional-grade video encoding to ensure the highest visual quality. You can choose the resolution that best fits your needs, whether you're sharing on social media or using it for high-end production.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="HD (1080p)">
    Perfect for most use cases, HD resolution delivers crisp, clear video that looks great on:
    - YouTube and social media platforms
    - Standard displays and monitors
    - Email sharing and web embedding
    - Mobile devices
    
    **Resolution**: 1920 x 1080 pixels
    
    **Best for**: Quick delivery, smaller file sizes, and broad compatibility
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="4K (2160p)">
    Maximum quality for professional productions and future-proofing:
    - High-end content production
    - 4K displays and televisions
    - Professional presentations
    - Archival purposes
    
    **Resolution**: 3840 x 2160 pixels
    
    **Best for**: Professional use, maximum detail, and premium quality
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
Both HD and 4K versions maintain the authentic Game of Thrones visual style, including the intricate details of the mechanical map and the signature opening sequence effects.
</Note>

## Choosing the right resolution

Consider these factors when selecting your video quality:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Intended platform" icon="tv">
    If you're uploading to YouTube, Instagram, or TikTok, HD is typically sufficient. For professional productions or large screens, choose 4K.
  </Card>
  <Card title="File size" icon="hard-drive">
    HD videos are smaller and faster to download and upload. 4K videos provide superior quality but require more storage and bandwidth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delivery time" icon="clock">
    HD videos render and deliver faster. 4K videos may take longer to process and send due to their larger file size.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Future use" icon="forward">
    If you plan to reuse your intro over time or on larger displays, 4K offers better longevity and flexibility.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
