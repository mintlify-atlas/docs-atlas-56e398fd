## Customization options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can I customize in the intro?">
    You can personalize three key elements:
    
    1. **Title text**: The main text that appears during the intro sequence
    2. **End text**: Additional text displayed at the conclusion of the video
    3. **Resolution**: Choose between HD (1080p) or 4K (2160p) quality
    
    The iconic Game of Thrones theme music and visual style remain consistent to preserve the authentic Westeros experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long can my custom text be?">
    While there are no strict character limits, shorter text works best for readability and visual impact. We recommend:
    - **Title text**: 1-5 words for optimal display
    - **End text**: 1-7 words to maintain the cinematic feel
    
    Longer text may appear smaller or less prominent in the final video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use special characters or emojis?">
    Standard text characters are fully supported, including letters, numbers, and common punctuation. For best results, stick to alphanumeric characters and basic symbols. Special Unicode characters or emojis may not render correctly in the final video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the music or visual style?">
    The intro maintains the authentic Game of Thrones theme music and visual aesthetic to preserve the iconic feel of the opening sequence. Customization focuses on the text elements to keep the experience true to the original series.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical details

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What video format will I receive?">
    Your personalized intro is delivered as an MP4 file, which is compatible with virtually all video editing software, social media platforms, and playback devices. MP4 provides an excellent balance of quality and file size.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between HD and 4K?">
    - **HD (1080p)**: 1920x1080 resolution, perfect for YouTube, social media, and most viewing scenarios. Smaller file size for easier uploading and sharing.
    - **4K (2160p)**: 3840x2160 resolution, ideal for professional projects, large screens, or when you need maximum detail and future-proofing.
    
    <Info>
    Choose 4K if you plan to crop, zoom, or do significant editing. The extra resolution gives you more flexibility in post-production.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long is the final video?">
    The intro video maintains the approximate length of the original Game of Thrones opening sequence, typically around 90 seconds. This duration provides the full cinematic experience while remaining practical for content intros.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I download the video multiple times?">
    Yes, the download link in your email remains active, so you can download your video whenever needed. We recommend saving a backup copy to your computer or cloud storage for future use.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my video email. What should I do?">
    Try these steps:
    
    1. **Check your spam/junk folder** - Email filters sometimes catch automated messages
    2. **Wait a bit longer** - During peak times, generation may take up to 30 minutes
    3. **Verify your email address** - Make sure you entered it correctly when submitting
    4. **Check for typos** - A small typo in your email means the video went to the wrong address
    
    If you still haven't received your video after 30 minutes, try submitting a new request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The video won't play on my device. What's wrong?">
    MP4 files should play on virtually all modern devices. If you're having trouble:
    
    - **Update your media player** - Make sure you're using the latest version
    - **Try a different player** - VLC Media Player (free) can play almost any video format
    - **Check your download** - The file may have been corrupted during download; try downloading again
    - **Verify file size** - 4K videos are large files; ensure your download completed fully
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The text in my video looks wrong. Can I fix it?">
    If your text doesn't appear as expected, you'll need to create a new video with corrected text. Before submitting:
    
    - Double-check spelling and capitalization
    - Keep text concise for better readability
    - Avoid excessive punctuation or special characters
    - Preview your text mentally in the Game of Thrones style
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund or remake if I'm not satisfied?">
    Since you specify all customization options before generation, please review your title text, end text, and resolution carefully before submitting. If you made an error in your text, you can create a new video with the correct information.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
